Welcome to Michigan Auto Spare Parts

Since 1999, Michigan Auto Spare Parts has been a trusted source for high-quality Japanese spare parts. With over two decades of experience, we specialize in providing a wide range of new, used, and reconditioned parts for all major Japanese car brands, including Toyota, Nissan, Honda, Mazda, and more.

Why Choose Us?

Extensive Inventory: We offer a vast selection of over 400,000 parts, from engines and transmissions to body parts and electrical components.
Quality Assurance: Every part is thoroughly inspected to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and reliability.
Global Shipping: We deliver to more than 200 countries, ensuring that you get the parts you need, wherever you are.
Expert Support: Our knowledgeable team is here to help you find the right parts for your vehicle, providing expert guidance and support in multiple languages.
